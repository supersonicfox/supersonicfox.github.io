What Does the Fox Wear?

Sep 4th, 2014

As part of doing Insanely Great Tennis for the local pros of Palo Alto, I came up with the idea of pitching a franchise to Shark Tank. To pitch a television show, you need a picture of yourself. I had my standard Linked-In profile, but that isn’t really very compelling. So I augmented my pitch with my tennis outfits, so it is a good time to blog about them :–)

My outfits came out of two issues:

a I frequently played tennis after biking (I biked with my kids to school, and then biked some more after). b To play tennis, you need Lots of fuzzy balls

With this two issues came my amazing epiphany: you should play tennis in biking shirts! And by you, I really mean you… as in everyone…. if you are reading this… go change your shirt and go play some tennis :–)

Another constraint on my outfits and clothing in general is that I do not want to think about clothing very much. So I have suits. My primary suits involve a red shirt and black pants. Sometimes I swap in a black shirt. That is it. Two choices. Mostly affected by the contents of the hamper.

Below you will see four outfits, which were initially just one, but broadened somewhat when the second and especially the third came along. And the fourth was actually the original “biking shirt”, but it has only one rear pocket, so I now just keep it around as a dry spare.

The Red Fox

This is the original. Worn with either a red (“Fear the Tree”) or black cap.

The Frisco Fox

This is a fun augmentation to the Red Fox. Their is a picture of the Golden Gate bridge on the shirt, and it promotes a good local bike shop. No one (sane) in or near San Francisco actually calls the city Frisco, but my slightly insane daughter likes to call it that, so this outfit’s name is for her.

The Zebra Fox

The above shirts were the top of the heap until this one came along. Having a shirt named after you is too awesome to not wear it. I need to get this in red though.

The Yoga Fox

This is a fun shirt, but just has one large pocket in the back, so isn’t as good as the ones above. For this one, I revealed my (lack of) hair.
