MRP

Nov 17th, 2014

Continuing my ‘insanity’ subtopic, this one is about how the world really works. Again, assuming you are not going to actually physically threaten me, there is a simply progression to how you can gain power over me. You need:

  • Money
  • Relationship

to get to the first level that I will call ‘Influence’. Yes, Money could be something other than actual money, but that would require you to know I like (for example) mangoes, gnocchi, and aquatic plants. So to keep things simple, assume it is money. How much money? A lot of money. Like tens of thousands of dollars. With that you have my attention. Others might be a bit less… and others might be even more. Money alone won’t work on me though, you have to have some kind of relationship with me. I have to know you and what your goals in general are. The stronger the relationship, the smaller the money might have to be. Or leveraging the relationship you might realize aquatic plants are very valuable to me.

OK, so you now have ‘Influence’… how do you get ‘Power’ over me? That part is simple too. You need to have a Purpose. For you. For me. For the Money. For the Relationship.

So in total you need:

  • Money
  • Relationship
  • Purpose

and if those are compelling to me, you have a lot of power over me. I will do a lot of things for you.

Politics and Lemmings

Venturing even further off from tennis. I live in a country that is basically insane. People go vote every couple years where voting in no way involves any of the above. Voting is “cute” but to actually impact things, you need

  • Money
  • Relationship
  • Purpose

and I am pretty sure no voting booth on the planet ever provided any of the above to any politician or political seat. And then to make things worse, these insane American voters then go onto Facebook and banter with all their friends about how bad things are. But again, none of that is doing MRP with the people that could actually change anything.

But they keep doing it year after year… election after election… instead of simply doing MRP. Sigh. Lemmings. Insane lemmings. Except they are the norm.
