Wilson Spin Clinic

Nov 23rd, 2014

Today I had an awesome time with adults and kids at a ‘spin clinic’ hosted by Wilson. Basically they want to show how swapping in one of their newer rackets makes your shots better. It was interesting to see other people do it (I was helping retrieve balls, so was effectively ‘receiving’ and watching pretty closely). The right way to do this is you hit you kind of normal 70% game speed balls, swap in a new racket, and compare. I am not normal, but I did try the ‘normal way’ and swapped in a wilson racket for my best HeadMP+Strings+57. Unfortunately for Wilson, their racket was not better.

But again, I was invited to this by Timi and was there to have fun and be helpful. For the helpful, I provided music, fresh orange juice, champagne, and a good ball retriever. For the fun, I did what any mage would do with a machine like that watching them. I hit my wickedest shots possible off every ball that was hit my way :–) And then wanted to see my stats.

The numbers:

  • Forehand-Topspin: 2500 RPM
  • Forehand-Slice: 4500 RPM ! :–)
  • Backhand-Topspin: 1500 RPM
  • Backhand-Slice: 3000 RPM

And my favorite number:

  • Furthest travel: 140 feet :–))

I also served, but they were packing up and I didn’t really get any numbers for it (too many kinds of serves and only 5 balls).

Great day. And I am headed to Carmel for the Prequalifier for the New Haven Qualifier for the New York Open. :–)

Thanks to John for the game and to Steve for keeping my should on.
